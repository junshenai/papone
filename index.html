About Poor Bunny

Poor Bunny is a fun and addictive adventure game where you control a cute bunny on a mission to collect carrots while avoiding various obstacles. With simple controls and increasingly challenging levels, it's perfect for players of all ages.

Navigate through colorful worlds, jump over gaps, avoid enemies, and collect as many carrots as you can to achieve high scores. The game features intuitive controls, vibrant graphics, and engaging gameplay that will keep you entertained for hours.

Game Features

  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Colorful and vibrant graphics
  • Increasingly challenging levels
  • Leaderboards to compete with friends
  • Regular updates with new content
